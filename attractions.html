Top Attractions

Soldarity Museum

This museum gives a history of communist times in Poland. It has many interactive exhibits, like riding a car during the era or going through communist era shops. The museum is three stories tall and you could spend a whole day wandering its halls.

Old Squares

Explore the historical old squares, where you can experience the rich cultural heritage and architectural beauty of Poland's past.

Holocaust Sites

Visit the Holocaust sites, including Auschwitz, to remember and honor the victims of one of the darkest periods in human history.
